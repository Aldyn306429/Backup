How to play Rock, Paper, or Scissors?

Rock, paper, scissors is a simple hand game where the player that plays the strongest “object” is the winner of the game. In the game, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. It's that easy! Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple game that anybody can play and win. There is no intrinsic advantage to the stronger, older, or more experienced opponent.

Directions:

In this mini-game, you will be playing against a computer, and when you click the icon, you'll use that object to duel against the computer. First to five points wins the game. At the end of this match, you can try again by clicking the "Another Match" button or, if it's enough for today, you can leave. I hope you have fun!
